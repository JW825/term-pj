Toystory 3

The saddest thing that all toys experience is that their owners grow up and stop playing with them. Woody and Buzz face this crisis, too. Andy goes to college and leaves home. The toys, which used to be nervous about separation, leave the house because of Andy's mother's mistake and end up being donated to the daycare center! Toys that Andy thinks have abandoned them. But they are extremely violent and tough. And the world of toys at daycare centers hidden in a conspiracy that they never imagined. Then, when they find out that Andy still loves them, the toy corps decides to take the biggest adventure of their lives to return to Andy's side. Toys that are crammed together around Woody. Will their great escape succeed!
